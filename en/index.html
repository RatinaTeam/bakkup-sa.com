About BAKKUP

At Bakkup we provide the broadest range of maintenance services across the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia in order to ensure that our clients have the highest asset availability, reliability and productivity across their entire industry. Providing cost- effective proposals and solutions to meet clients’ budget and requirements is our ultimate goal to achieve.

Our Vision

 We strive as a company to provide complete industrial maintenance services with quality, added value and facility solutions. We measure our success in partnership between the growth of our company and the satisfaction and growth of our customers.

Our Commitment

Worry-free maintenance issues concerning industrial and commercial clients in kingdom of Saudi Arabia. We make this possible by offering our wide range of quality maintenance services with certified teams and Operational Standards as well as designed Computerized Maintenance Management Systems (CMMS).

Our Team

Bakkup employs experienced professionals led by a highly qualified management team that understands the maintenance industry well. The team provides a 24/7 service and ensures prompt on-site response.
